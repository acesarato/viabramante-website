Meet the Artist

Illustrator and surface pattern designer Alessandra Cesarato was born in Venice, Italy and began her artistic career in London
painting cels for animated features. She is also a practiced linguist and educator,
and a blog writer for a popular art community.

She creates art that portrays recognizable and intriguing subjects using graceful and elegant colour palettes. "I describe my style as understated and eclectic, inspired by everyday scenes, urbane and naturalistic subjects that can resonate with viewers and art lovers".
