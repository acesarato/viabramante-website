Meet The Artist

Born in Venice, Italy, illustrator and surface pattern designer Alessandra Cesarato began her artistic career in London
painting cels for popular animated features. She is also a practiced linguist and educator, and a blog writer.

Alessandra creates art that portrays recognizable subjects using graceful and elegant colour palettes.
Her understated style and eclectic designs range from the naturalistic to the everyday and the urbane.
