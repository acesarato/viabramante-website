Meet the Artist

Born in Venice, Italy, illustrator and surface pattern designer Alessandra Cesarato began her artistic career in London painting cels for popular animated features. She is also a practiced linguist and educator, and a blog writer

Alessandra creates art that portrays recognizable subjects using graceful and elegant colour palettes. With an understated and eclectic style her work ranges from the naturalistic to the everyday and urbane with which she wishes to resonates with all kinds of art lovers.
