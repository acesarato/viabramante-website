Meet the Artist

Illustrator and surface pattern designer Alessandra Cesarato, is the artist behind Viabramante and draws inspiration from her many interests and experiences.
Born in Venice, Italy, but living in London since the 1980's she began her artistic career painting cels for animated features.
She is also a practiced linguist and educator, and a blog writer for a popular art community.

"I create art that portrays recognizable subjects hoping to intrigue and resonate with viewers and art lovers. I describe my style as understated and eclectic, depicting everyday scenes, urbane and naturalistic subjects, trying to use graceful and elegant colour palettes.
