viabramante

Illustration and Print Design
by Alessandra Cesarato

viabramante's designs are a mix of understated, quirky and timeless subjects inspired by the natural world and ordinary aspects of life

My pattern designs and illustrations depict the simplicity and enchanting beauty of the natural world and the everyday. I seek to capture the allure of the ordinary with a focus on elegant forms and colours..


I create art that portrays recognizable subjects in a subtle and personal manner. With an eye towards refined and graceful color palettes, my “urbane and naturalistic” themes portray the conventional and unconventional
in a style which aims to resonate with viewers.




Meet the Artist

viabramante is the illustration and print design endeavour
of London-based artist Alessandra Cesarato.

Available for commissions and collaborations for illustration and surface pattern design licensing
Please contact Alessandra to discuss projects and to view her full portfolio

Alessandra's art is inspired by the enchanting beauty of the natural world
and the allure hidden within ordinary, timeless, and everyday subjects.

Alessandra describes her style as understated and eclectic, depicting everyday scenes and urbane and naturalistic subjects
using graceful and elegant color palettes.
"I create art that portrays recognizable subjects
hoping to intrigue and resonate with viewers and art lovers.

The more I paint the more I like everything. J M Basquiat.
