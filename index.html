Meet the Artist

Alessandra's art is inspired by the enchanting beauty of the natural world and the allure hidden within ordinary, timeless, and everyday subjects

Alessandra describes her style as understated and eclectic, depicting everyday scenes and urbane and naturalistic subjects, using graceful and elegant color palettes.

Illustrator and surface pattern designer Alessandra Cesarato, draws her inspiration from her many interests and past experiences. Born in Venice, Italy, but living in London since the 1980's she began her artistic career painting cels for animated features. She is also a xxxx linguist and educator, and a blog writer for a popular art community.
"I create art that portrays recognizable subjects hoping to intrigue and resonate with viewers and art lovers".
